Aesthetically brilliant and technically precise, the beauty and discipline behind mandir architecture enjoys a long and celebrated history in Hinduism. Few places reach so far into the heart of the Hindu faith and into the faith of the individual Hindu. Presented here is a brief insight into how mandirs originated and developed, and the rigorous scriptural prescriptions for creating a traditional Hindu mandir.

Hostels Structure

Educational Hostels provide students with an environment that is conducive to academic success, supplemented by the support resources students need. Modern facilities and quality dining services provide the basics at these educational hostels, but it is the academic and residential mentoring programs that really make them stand out. Students are also provided with opportunities to pursue their extracurricular interests in music, theatre and sports. Through over 50 years of service, BAPS has developed experience and a nuanced understanding of how to best support students through their formative years in secondary and higher education.
